# Kibbutznik Landing & First-Visit Design Review

## Executive Summary

The product is technically solid, but a cold HN visitor hits a series of credibility leaks and broken promises in the first 30 seconds. The two highest-impact problems are factual/trust failures verified live: the welcome page's highlight reel tells visitors to "read the actual artifact" yet every card dead-ends at the generic viewer root, and the viewer's full-screen play overlay — the literal first thing a visitor reads — claims "Six AI members" when the community has 10. A pervasive secondary theme is leaked internal jargon (`OutThere`, `OnTheAir`, `support_pulse`, `ProposeArtifact`, unexplained "age"/"seniority") surfacing in user-facing copy across all three surfaces despite correct label maps already existing in the same files. Accessibility (WCAG contrast failures on every primary button, no keyboard focus rings on landing) and brand fragmentation across pages round out the list; these matter less for the stay/leave decision but are cheap to fix and several are real WCAG violations.

## P0 — Trust/credibility failures a first-time visitor hits immediately

### 1. Highlight reel promises deep-links; all cards land on viewer root
**Location:** `landing/welcome.html:863` (copy), highlights API
**Evidence (verified live):** `curl /kbz/highlights?limit=6` — all six items return `link":"/kbz/viewer/"`. Copy says "Click any to read the actual artifact." A visitor clicking "Values & Purpose" lands on the generic viewer and finds nothing specific.
**Recommendation:** One-line fix now — change the section lead to "Click any to watch the kibbutz live" and drop the "read the actual artifact" promise. Longer term, add per-proposal deep-links (`/kbz/viewer/?proposal=<id>`) and wire them through the API.

### 2. Viewer play overlay says "Six AI members" — community has 10
**Location:** `viewer/app.js:1904`
**Evidence (verified live):** overlay text reads "Six AI members will propose, debate, and decide"; `/kbz/simulation/status` returns `member_count:10`. This is the first sentence a paused-sim visitor reads, and HN readers check claims.
**Recommendation:** Replace the hardcoded "Six" with `status.community.member_count` (already fetched on mount), or drop the number: "AI members will propose, debate, and decide."

### 3. White-on-accent primary buttons fail WCAG AA (3.83:1) on every surface
**Location:** `landing/welcome.html:323-327` (.btn-primary), `app/style.css:103-107` (.btn.primary), `viewer/style.css:913-921` (.send-btn)
**Evidence:** white text on `--accent #e94560` = 3.83:1 at 15px/600; AA requires 4.5:1. Affects "Open the app," "Send me a magic link," "Support pulse," "Send."
**Recommendation:** Darken the accent to ~`#c8293e` (gives 4.6:1 with white). This is the single most prominent interaction on every page and the fix also improves link-text contrast.

### 4. Viewer `--text-muted: #666` fails AA across the dark UI
**Location:** `viewer/style.css:17`, used at `:592`, `:636`, `:653` and throughout
**Evidence:** 2.18–2.97:1 on the dark backgrounds (AA needs 4.5:1), applied to metadata at 0.7rem — author lines, timestamps, proposal meta. This is the data a first-time viewer reads to understand what's happening.
**Recommendation:** Map `--text-muted` uses to `--text-secondary` (#999) or raise to ~`#8a9ab5`.

*Note on memory.html (raised as P0 by one agent): genuinely off-brand (dark neon palette, "KBZ" naming, no OG/analytics, links back to index.html) but a deep technical page few cold HN visitors will land on. Downgrading to P1 — see #11.*

## P1 — High-impact polish a visitor will notice while deciding

### 5. Internal status jargon leaks into user-facing UI (multiple sites, label maps already exist)
This is one root cause with several surfaces; fix together. In every case a correct label map (`PROPOSAL_STATUS_LABEL`, `PROPOSAL_TYPE_META`) already exists in the same file and is bypassed.
- **App proposal detail modal** — `app/app.js:1988` renders `{proposal.proposal_status}` raw ("OutThere"); verified. Also member-history panel `app/app.js:2579`.
- **Viewer proposal board column headers** — `viewer/app.js:3509`/`3514` render "On The Air"/"Out There" (verified) while cards inside show "Active vote"/"Open for support" — visibly inconsistent.
- **Viewer detail panel badge** — `viewer/app.js:567` raw status; mini-badges at `:1088,1105,1286,1353,1707`.
- **Viewer news ticker** — `viewer/app.js:4456-4466` emits "JoinAction proposal accepted" instead of "Join action…" via `PROPOSAL_TYPE_META`.
- **App success toast** — `app/app.js:3320` "...see it in OutThere."
- **App dashboard applications list** — `app/app.js:991` interpolates raw `a.status`.

**Recommendation:** Wrap each with the existing label map: `PROPOSAL_STATUS_LABEL[s] || s` (status) and `PROPOSAL_TYPE_META[t]?.label || t` (type). Keep raw value in a `title=` attr for developers.

### 6. Title/OG/h1 lead with "pulse-based direct democratic engine" three times
**Location:** `landing/welcome.html:6` (title), `:17` (og:title), `:834` (h1) — all verified identical
**Evidence:** the most-shared surface's first impression is an opaque phrase. Contrast `guide.html`'s legible meta and the app's own h1 ("Run your community by pulse, not politics").
**Recommendation:** Lead with benefit — "Kibbutznik — group decisions without admins or drama" or reuse "Run your community by pulse, not politics." Keep the mechanism phrase for the sub-header.

### 7. "kibbutzim/kibbutz" used before definition
**Location:** `landing/welcome.html:861, 863, 1026`
**Recommendation:** Use "communities" in the highlights heading; introduce "kibbutz (a self-governing community)" parenthetically on first use. Brand name "Kibbutznik" is fine as-is.

### 8. og:url points to /welcome.html; no canonical
**Location:** `landing/welcome.html:19` (verified); `/` and `/welcome.html` are byte-identical (same ETag `6a146ad6-bc12`, 48146 bytes)
**Recommendation:** Set `og:url` to `https://kibbutznik.org/` and add `<link rel="canonical" href="https://kibbutznik.org/">`.

### 9. Hero has three near-equal CTAs
**Location:** `landing/welcome.html:844-851`
**Recommendation:** Drop "Read the friendly guide" from the hero (it's in nav + footer) or demote it to an underlined text link, leaving "Open the app" (primary) + "See a community in action" (soft).

### 10. "How it works" and "Not a forum. Not a DAO." explain the same three mechanics twice
**Location:** `landing/welcome.html:874-925` vs `970-1019`; hooks repeated at `:842` and `:974`
**Recommendation:** Merge into one section — keep the friendlier feature cards, absorb the sharper differentiation framing into each card body, remove the standalone pillars section. Shorter page, one clean explanation.

### 11. Brand fragmentation: memory.html / index.html / viewer are design islands
**Location:** `landing/memory.html` (dark neon palette, "KBZ" in title/topbar/footer, no OG, no Plausible, links to index.html); `landing/index.html` (all-caps dark "KIBBUTZNIK", surfaced as "Technical landing"/"Psychedelic edition" in footers of guide/ecosystem/welcome); viewer dark theme unframed.
**Recommendation:** Decide whether index.html is retired (redirect `/` to canonical) or kept as a labeled "developer edition." Restyle or explicitly brand memory.html; fix its back-link to `/welcome.html`; replace user-facing "KBZ" with "Kibbutznik." Add a "Live simulation — Kibbutznik" eyebrow to the viewer to connect the brand across the cream→navy jump.

### 12. Inter font declared everywhere but never loaded
**Location:** `app/style.css:31`, `welcome.html:59`, `guide.html:48`, `ecosystem.html:49`, `contact.html:30`; no `@font-face`/CDN link anywhere
**Recommendation:** Add one shared `<link>` to a self-hosted/CDN Inter (Bunny or rsms.me). Currently silently falls through to system-ui on Windows/Linux/Android.

### 13. No redirect-after-login; user loses the community they came from
**Location:** `app/app.js:597` (unconditional `navigate("#/dashboard")`), trigger at `:1427`; same for InviteClaimPage `:3464`
**Recommendation:** Store `window.location.hash` in sessionStorage before redirecting to login; read and navigate back post-verify.

### 14. Invite claim asks already-signed-in users to re-type email
**Location:** `app/app.js:3440` (no `user` prop), `:3744` (not passed)
**Recommendation:** Pass `user` to InviteClaimPage; when present, show "Accept — join as {user_name}" instead of the email form.

### 15. Landing pages have no :focus-visible (keyboard nav invisible)
**Location:** `welcome.html` and `index.html` — verified zero focus-visible rules (app/style.css has 2)
**Recommendation:** Add `a:focus-visible, button:focus-visible { outline: 2px solid var(--pulse); outline-offset: 2px; }`. Also convert index.html's accordion `div.guide-card-head onclick` (`:1587`) to `<button>` — currently keyboard-inaccessible primary content.

### 16. App modals lack dialog semantics
**Location:** `app/app.js:1975, 1573, 2493`; close button `:1991` has no aria-label
**Recommendation:** Add `role="dialog" aria-modal="true" aria-labelledby`, `aria-label="Close"` on ×, and focus management on open/close.

### 17. "age N" shown without unit (means pulse rounds, not days)
**Location:** app `app.js:1725, 2010`; viewer `app.js:573, 3448`
**Recommendation:** Render "N pulses old" with a tooltip explaining one pulse = one decision round. Most domain-specific number on the page; opaque as bare "age 3."

## P2 — Cheap wins, lower first-impression leverage

- **Surface live community count.** `/kbz/highlights` returns `total_public_roots:206` (verified) but it's not shown. Inject "Across 206 communities…" into the welcome highlights lead (`landing/welcome.html:1112-1144`) — real, live social proof.
- **`support_pulse` jargon in glossary tooltip** — `app/app.js:2103` (verified): the Pulse glossary says members call "support_pulse" but the button says "⚡ Support pulse." Mirror the button label.
- **Artifacts empty state names nonexistent type** — `app/app.js:2812` says "File a ProposeArtifact proposal"; real type is `CreateArtifact` (`:2233`). One-word fix.
- **Mobile tab strip wraps instead of scrolling** — `app/app.js:1444` uses `className="row"` (verified) which wraps at ≤640px; the purpose-built `.tab-strip` (`style.css:465-477`, overflow-x:auto + fade mask) exists but is unused. Swap the className.
- **Babel transpiles in-browser on every cold load** — `app/index.html:40,45`: ~850KB gzipped Babel + 3,789-line JSX parsed at runtime; ~4s on 3G before first paint. Structural, but pre-transpiling with esbuild (<1s build) or at least adding `<link rel=preload>` + `crossorigin` to the Babel tag would materially help mobile first paint.
- **Pulse-organism SVG runs ~49 animations** — `landing/welcome.html:706-797`: add `@media (max-width:640px){.site-bg{display:none}}` (it's clipped/invisible at mobile width anyway) to drop compositor layers on the devices most likely to feel it.
- **App lacks `<main>` landmark** — `app/app.js:3768-3783`: wrap content in `<main id="main-content">` + skip-link.
- **Login/invite email inputs have no `<label>`** — `app/app.js:613, 3491, 3611` (placeholder only); the create-kibbutz/profile forms at `:1147` show the correct wrapping-label pattern.
- **Viewer/memory/error pages missing Plausible** — absent from `memory.html`, `404.html`, `500.html`, `viewer/index.html`; breaks measurement continuity.
- **Welcome highlight-type badges fail contrast** — `landing/welcome.html:435-449`: sage 2.35:1, statement 3.34:1; `--gold-soft` referenced but undefined in `:root`. Darken foregrounds and define the missing var.
- **DelegateArtifact wants a raw UUID** — `app/app.js:2265-2270`: add `pickFrom:"actions"` to match JoinAction's dropdown pattern.
- **Comments locked on decided proposals** — `app/app.js:1929`: `canComment = imMember && canSupport` blocks post-decision discussion; split to `canComment = !!imMember` and gate only Reply/Support on active status.
- **Seniority shown bare** — `app/app.js:782, 1501, 2519`: add a Term tooltip ("pulse rounds participated in").
- **Link guide.html from inside the app** — `guide.html` is the product's strongest copy but the app only reaches it via Term tooltips; add "New? Read the 8-minute guide →" to the app LandingPage (`app.js:494`) and the empty-community view.
- **Viewer tab groups / intro banner** — group names (Live/People/Work/Governance) defined in TAB_GROUPS but never rendered (`viewer/app.js:3124`); ViewerIntroBanner only renders inside DashboardTab (`:3613`). Render group labels and lift the banner above tab content.
- **"KBZ" acronym unexplained** — `landing/index.html:1180,1298,1579,1901`, `memory.html:6,335,641`: replace user-facing "KBZ" with "Kibbutznik."
- **"bounded ~100-step run" overlay copy** — `viewer/app.js:1903-1907`: implies a finite demo; reframe as "the sim pauses after each run — press play to continue."

**Dropped as already-fixed/not-reproduced:** ProposalCard list status labels (confirmed correctly using the label map). **Flagged for judgment, not a defect:** the viewer "Big Brother / Dark Surveillance" naming (`viewer/app.js:3902`) — intentional but arguably off-brand for a deliberative-democracy tool; worth a product decision, not a bug.
